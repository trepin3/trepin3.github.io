## 1. Google Ads Campaign Strategy

### Campaign Type: Call-Only Campaigns

Call-only campaigns are optimized to drive phone calls, not website clicks. For a local service business, phone calls convert at 3-5x the rate of form submissions.

**Setup Steps:**
1. Go to [ads.google.com](https://ads.google.com) ‚Üí New Campaign ‚Üí Leads ‚Üí Phone Calls
2. Set your business phone number (must be verified)
3. Set location targeting: Arizona (or specific cities below)
4. Set language: English
5. Set bidding: Maximize conversions (start here, switch to Target CPA after 30 conversions)

### Budget & Bidding

| Setting | Recommendation |
|---------|---------------|
| Daily budget | $15-25/day to start ($450-750/month) |
| Expected CPC | $3-8 per click |
| Expected call cost | $15-40 per call |
| Target calls/day | 3-8 |
| Conversion rate | 20-35% of calls book a shoot |

### Ad Schedule

- **Active days:** Monday through Saturday
- **Active hours:** 7:00 AM - 7:00 PM MST
- **Bid boost +25%:** 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM (when agents are most active planning listings)
- **Off:** Sundays (low real estate activity)

### Conversion Tracking Setup

1. In Google Ads, go to Tools ‚Üí Conversions ‚Üí New Conversion Action
2. Select "Phone calls" ‚Üí "Calls from ads or a website"
3. Set call duration threshold: 60 seconds (filters out accidental clicks)
4. Install the Google Ads tag on your website (already templated in index.html, just uncomment and add your ID)
5. Add the conversion action ID to script.js where marked with `AW-XXXXXXXXXX/YYYYYYY`

### Optimization Tips

- **Week 1-2:** Let campaigns run without changes to gather data
- **Week 3:** Pause keywords with >$50 spend and 0 conversions
- **Week 4:** Add top-performing search terms as exact match keywords
- **Monthly:** Review search terms report, add negatives, adjust bids
- **After 30 conversions:** Switch to Target CPA bidding

## 2. Free Advertising Strategies

### Google Business Profile (Highest Priority ‚Äî Do This First)

Your Google Business Profile (GBP) is the single most impactful free marketing channel. It shows up in Google Maps and the local 3-pack above organic results.

**Setup Steps:**
1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
2. Create profile for "Merchant Skyview Media"
3. **Category:** Primary: "Photographer" | Additional: "Aerial Photographer", "Real Estate Photographer"
4. **Service area:** List all 12 cities (Phoenix, Scottsdale, Tucson, Mesa, Chandler, Gilbert, Tempe, Surprise, Peoria, Goodyear, Flagstaff, Sedona)
5. **Description:** "Professional drone photography and videography for Arizona real estate. FAA Part 107 certified, $1M insured. Aerial photos, videos, 360 virtual tours, and construction progress documentation. 24-hour delivery. Serving Phoenix, Scottsdale, Tucson, and all of Arizona."
6. **Photos:** Upload 10+ portfolio photos (aerial shots), your logo, and team photos
7. **Hours:** Mon-Sat 7AM-7PM, Sun Closed

- Ask every satisfied client for a Google review (send link via text immediately after delivery)
- Respond to every review within 24 hours
- Update photos monthly with new work
- Use Google Posts for seasonal promotions or new service announcements

## 7. Arizona Market Analysis

### City-by-City Breakdown

| City | Strategy | Avg Home Price | Notes |
|------|----------|---------------|-------|
| **Phoenix** | Volume play | $400-500K | Largest market, most agents, most competition. Cast wide net. |
| **Scottsdale** | Premium/luxury | $700K-2M+ | Higher prices = higher willingness to pay for premium packages. Lead with $599 Premium. |
| **Tucson** | Less competition | $300-400K | Fewer drone photographers. Easier to dominate. Good early market. |
| **Mesa** | Suburban/new construction | $400-500K | Lots of new builds. Good for construction progress + residential combo. |
| **Chandler** | Suburban families | $450-550K | Growing market. Good for Standard package. |
| **Gilbert** | New construction | $450-600K | Rapidly growing. Target new home builders + agents. |
| **Tempe** | Mixed (condos + homes) | $350-500K | Near ASU. Smaller residential market but good for commercial. |
| **Surprise/Peoria/Goodyear** | Emerging suburbs | $350-450K | Growing fast. Less competition. Get in early. |
| **Flagstaff** | Seasonal/niche | $500-700K | Mountain properties look amazing from above. Seasonal (spring-fall). |
| **Sedona** | Luxury/tourism | $700K-1.5M+ | Stunning landscape = stunning aerials. Premium pricing justified. |

### Seasonal Strategy

**Peak Season (October ‚Äì May):**
- Real estate market is most active
- Snowbirds arrive, buying activity peaks
- Run full Google Ads budget
- Network aggressively
- This is when 70% of your revenue will come

**Slow Season (June ‚Äì September):**
- 110¬∞F+ heat reduces outdoor shoots
- Shoot early morning (5-8 AM) to avoid heat and get golden hour
- Pivot to construction progress work (builders don't stop for summer)
- Reduce Google Ads budget by 40-50%
- Focus on content creation, portfolio building, relationship nurturing
- Offer "summer specials" to maintain some volume
